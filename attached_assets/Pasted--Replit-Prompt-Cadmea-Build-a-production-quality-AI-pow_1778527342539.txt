# Replit Prompt — Cadmea

Build a production-quality AI-powered urban decision platform called **Cadmea**.

Cadmea is a smart interactive city intelligence platform designed for Lithuania.
The goal is to help citizens, families, students, tourists, and expats make better decisions about where to live, travel, study, or invest using real public data combined with AI analysis.

Cadmea is NOT just a map.
It is an **AI-powered urban decision engine**.

Users should be able to ask natural-language questions like:

* “Where is the safest area for families in Vilnius?”
* “Which districts have the cleanest air and lowest noise?”
* “What is the best affordable area near public transport?”
* “Where should tourists stay without needing a car?”
* “Which areas have the best schools and green spaces?”
* “Find places with low light pollution and good healthcare access.”

The AI should instantly analyze real Lithuanian public datasets and respond with:

* interactive map highlights,
* district rankings,
* AI-generated explanations,
* livability scores,
* comparisons,
* charts,
* and recommendations.

The platform must feel polished, premium, modern, trustworthy, intelligent, eco-friendly, and Scandinavian-inspired.
